Our Love Story

Introduction

20 August 2022

Our first time laying eyes on one another was outside of Liam's flat. Deandra said this will be her husband one day.

First Date (& Kiss)

26 August 2022

Liam took Deandra to The Archive, a car detailing shop with delicious coffee. Thereafter, trip to the planetarium followed by a lunch at Ocean Basket in Camps Bay. It was only a few hours later the first kiss was initiated by Liam.

Asked out

10 September 2022

It was a mere 3 weeks after we first met where Liam asked Deandra out in a canola field outside Durbanville.

Proposal

09 August 2024

Liam asked Deandra to become his wife within 200 meters of the location where he asked her out. Once again in a Canola field.

Fun fact, neither of us has any profound liking to Canola fields.

Where will the wedding be held?

What should I wear? Is there a dress code?

Semi-formal. There is no dedicated color pallet.

Indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be held outdoors with the reception indoors.

What happens after the ceremony?

Guests enjoy some appetizers, drinks and conversation. The newlyweds will be signing their contract and having their couples shoot.

Between 14h15 and 14h45. Note, there is an off-road section on the way to the venue, please allocate an extra 5 to 10 minutes to your drive. Any late comers will unfortunately miss the ceremony.

Where should guests park?

Parking is available at the location.

Are kids welcome?

Although we love children, and we know you love your own, we have decided this will be a child-free wedding. Thank you for understanding.

Can I bring a plus one?

No, unless stated on your invitation.
